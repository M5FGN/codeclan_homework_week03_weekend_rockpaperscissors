Rules of the Game

Two Players

  1. Player 1 enter your name in the text box.
  2. Choose what move you want to play.
  3. Player 2 enter your name in the text box.
  4. Choose what move you want to play.
  5. Click Play
  6. The winner will be displayed.

One Player vrs the Computer

  1. Player 1 enter your name in the text box.
  2. Choose what move you want to play.
  3. Click Play
  4. The computer will randomly choose a move and the winner will be displayed.

Who Wins?

  • Paper beats Rock
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
